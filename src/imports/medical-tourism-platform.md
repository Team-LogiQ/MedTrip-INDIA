**Prompt: Build an AI-Powered Medical Tourism Recommendation Platform (India Only)**

Create a web-based platform designed for **international patients seeking medical treatment in India**. The platform should help users find the **best hospitals in India for their disease or treatment**, along with **estimated treatment costs and hospital stay expenses**.

The goal is to create a **trusted medical tourism assistant** that helps patients easily discover the right hospitals, understand the total cost of treatment, and plan their medical journey to India.

---

# Core Concept

The platform works as a **medical recommendation and cost estimation system**.

Users will enter their **disease, treatment, or medical problem** in the platform.
The system then recommends:

• Top hospitals in India for that treatment
• Estimated treatment cost
• Doctor consultation cost
• Surgery/procedure cost
• Medicine and recovery cost
• **5-day hospital stay cost**
• Total estimated medical trip cost

The goal is to give patients **complete transparency before planning their medical trip**.

---

# User Flow

1. User visits the homepage.

2. User selects or enters their **disease/treatment**.

3. User optionally selects a **preferred hospital** from the hospital dropdown.

4. The system analyzes the request and displays:

   • Recommended hospitals in India
   • Estimated treatment cost
   • Hospital stay cost (5 days)
   • Recovery information
   • Total estimated trip cost

5. The user can then:

   • Request consultation
   • Contact hospital
   • Download treatment cost estimate
   • Plan medical trip

# GENIUS FEATURE (Hackathon Differentiator)

Implement an **AI Medical Journey Planner**.

This feature automatically generates a **visual treatment timeline** for the patient after selecting a treatment.

Example Timeline:

Day 1–2 → Online consultation with doctor
Day 3 → Arrival in India + hospital admission
Day 4 → Medical tests & diagnosis
Day 5 → Surgery / Procedure
Day 6–10 → Recovery period in hospital (5-day stay)
Day 11–14 → Local recovery + follow-up consultation
Day 15 → Safe return travel

The system should display this as a **visual timeline or progress journey** showing the entire medical trip from consultation to recovery.

This feature helps patients clearly understand **what their treatment journey will look like**, which greatly improves trust and planning.
